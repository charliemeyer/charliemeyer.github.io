About Me

I like math, coding, the New England Patriots, learning new stuff, teaching other people new stuff, music, and It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia. I've been playing clarinet for ten years, singing in the Tufts Gospel Choir for three, and I listen to any music a streaming service will send my way. When it comes to programming, Python is my scripting language of choice, but I'm not afraid to use C if it's what's needed to get the job done. I'm a member of Tufts Futurism Society, and hope that I can somehow help the future get here quicker with whatever it is I do. My favorite colors are grey and blue.

Auto Sched

  • Solution for scheduling people for work shifts.
  • Has a web front end running on Google App Engine for collecting worker availability data
  • Optimal schedules are approximated using a probabilistic algorithm implemented in Python. (finding an optimal schedule is NP hard)
  • Built by me to help speed up my TA scheduling duties as Comp40 teaching fellow.
