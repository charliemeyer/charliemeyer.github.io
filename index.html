About Me

As a lifelong Massachusetts resident, I am a diehard Patriots fan and habitual Dunkin Donuts visitor. I've played clarinet for eleven years, so if you need a medium quality second clarinetist for your wind ensemble, let me know (seriously). Also, if you need an OK baritone singer for a choir or DJ for a radio show, those are things I am provably capable of doing from my time in the Tufts Third Day Gospel Choir and at WMFO, Tufts Freeform Radio.

I study math and computer science at Tufts and you can usually find me in Halligan Hall working as a teaching assistant for Tufts Comp40. In summers between years at Tufts, I've spent time campaigning for MA State Rep Josh Cutler, redesigning the GENI Portal (geni.net) at BBN Technologies, and coding on Microsoft SQL Server in Redmond, WA. In Fall 2017, I'll start work at Palantir Technologies in New York City as a forward deployed software engineer, which I'm very excited about.
